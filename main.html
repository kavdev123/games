FAQ

            

                

                    
Q: What is a Steam AppID?

                    

                        A Steam AppID is a unique identifier assigned to each game or software on the Steam platform. It is used to access specific content and features related to that game or software.
                    

                

                

                    
Q: How do I find the AppID of a game?

                    

                        You can find the AppID of a game by visiting its store page on Steam and looking at the URL. The AppID is the number at the end of the URL. For example, in the URL https://store.steampowered.com/app/220968, the AppID is 220968.
                    

                

                

                    
Q: What formats are the manifests available in?

                    

                        Manifests are primarily available in the .acf format. If a .json version is available, it will be provided as an alternative download option.
                    

                

                

                    
Q: Is my data safe?

                    

                        Yes, your data is safe. We do not store or log any personal data. All checks are performed locally in your browser.
